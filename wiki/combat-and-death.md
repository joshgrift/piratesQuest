# Combat and Death

Combat is risky, expensive, and often worth it.

You fight from your ship by firing broadside cannons at enemies on the sea.

## How Combat Works

Fire your cannons like this:

- **Q** fires the left side
- **E** fires the right side

Combat consumes cannonballs from your inventory and has a 2-second cooldown between shots.

## Who You Fight

### AI Traders

AI traders drop the goods they are carrying. They are a fast way to gain cargo without visiting a port.

### AI Pirates

AI pirates carry stolen loot and can sometimes provide rare resources.

### AI Fleets

AI fleets are the highest-risk targets. They guard valuable cargo, including rare resources and components.

Defeating a fleet usually requires cooperation.

### Other Players

When you sink another player, they drop half of their inventory.

## Death Penalties

Death is punishing, but not permanent.

When you die:

- you lose **half** of all items in your inventory
- you lose **all** equipped components
- you respawn after a short delay at a random location with basic supplies

## How To Reduce Losses

Before a dangerous trip:

- repair your ship
- carry enough cannonballs
- keep useful resources on hand
- store valuables in a vault

The vault is especially important because its contents survive death.

## A Simple Rule

Do not treat every enemy as a mandatory fight.

Sometimes the best combat decision is:

- leave early
- bank valuables first
- return with a better ship or allies

That is still winning.
