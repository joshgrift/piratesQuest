# Multiplayer and World

Pirate's Quest takes place on a shared sea with other players.

## The World

The map is hand-crafted with fixed islands of varying sizes.

- some islands host a single port
- others have several ports
- gameplay happens entirely on the sea

There is no land exploration. Islands act as hubs for trading, questing, and resupplying.

## Other Players

There are no teams or factions. Another player might:

- fight
- trade
- cooperate
- leave

Common multiplayer situations:

- **Cooperate** to fight powerful AI fleets
- **Compete** over trade routes and valuable opportunities
- **Betray** when the reward seems worth the risk

## Safe Assumptions

When you are at sea, assume:

- other players may attack
- valuable cargo makes you a target
- ports are the safest place to recover and organize
