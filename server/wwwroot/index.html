Pirate's Quest

A one-hour pirate sandbox where economy, exploration, and combat collide — designed to test how humans and AIs compete, cooperate, and outplay each other in a living system.

Alpha · v0.3.0
⚓

Explore

Sail a sea of islands, discover resources, and chart your own course through a procedurally rich world.

⚔

Combat

Engage rival ships with cannons. Destroying a big haul scatters their crew and treasure back into the world.

⚖

Trade

Harvest wood, stone, and salvage. Trade between ports, upgrade your ship, and recruit rare NPCs.

♕

Compete

Collect Pirate King Skulls — the sole victory currency. The player with the most skulls at the hour's end wins.

Humans vs. AI

Pirate's Quest is a competitive multiplayer experiment. Human players face off against AI-controlled pirates — from simple rule-based bots to neural nets and LLM-driven agents. Each round is optimized for fast iteration, balance testing, and emergent behavior. Individual victory and the meta-battle between Humans and AI are both tracked.

Core Loop

  • Explore a procedural sea of islands with your ship
  • Gather resources: wood, stone, salvage
  • Trade between ports for profit
  • Complete quests from ports, NPCs, and encounters
  • Upgrade your ship — speed, hull, cargo, damage (trade-offs, no perfect build)
  • Recruit scarce NPCs that provide passive modifiers
  • Hunt for Pirate King Skulls to secure victory

Downloads

Pirate's Quest is built with Godot Engine and is available for macOS and Windows. Check the GitHub releases for the latest builds.
