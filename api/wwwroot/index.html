Set Sail

How to Play

Command your pirate ship across a sea filled with islands, resources, ports, and rival captains. Gather riches, upgrade your vessel, and buy trophies to prove you're the greatest pirate on the water.

Sail & Explore

Navigate with W/S to control speed and A/D to steer. Discover islands, find resource collection points marked in green, and dock at ports marked in red.

Broadside Combat

Fire cannons left with Q and right with E. Sink enemy ships and their loot scatters into the water for you to collect. Watch your health and cannon reload status.

Trade at Ports

Each port buys and sells different goods at different prices. Harvest tea, fish, wood, and iron from collection points, then sell high at the right port. Buy low, sell high — a pirate's guide to economics.

Claim Victory

Buy trophies at ports with your hard-earned gold. The pirate with the most trophies when the round ends is crowned the winner. Every decision matters in this one-hour showdown.

The Pirate's Path

Core Loop

Each round is a tense one-hour race. Here's how every great pirate captain rises to glory.

Harvest

Sail to green markers near islands and in open water to collect tea, fish, wood, and iron.

Trade

Dock at red-marked ports. Each port has different prices — buy low, sell high across islands.

Upgrade

Use gold and resources to buy ship components. Equip up to four — every build has trade-offs.

Fight

Sink rival pirates and loot their floating cargo. Destroyed ships drop everything they carried.

Win

Spend gold on trophies at ports. Most trophies at the end of the round wins the game.

The Experiment

Humans vs. AI

Pirate's Quest is more than a game — it's a competitive experiment. Human players face off against AI-controlled pirates, from simple rule-based bots to neural nets and LLM-driven agents.

Each round is optimized for fast iteration, balance testing, and emergent behavior. Individual victories and the broader meta-battle between Humans and AI are both tracked on the leaderboard.

A sandbox server is always available for testing builds and experimenting with the botting platform.
